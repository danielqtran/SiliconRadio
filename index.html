FLAVOURS OF THE EAST W/ ALBOE & MERAK

Alboe and Merak showcase the most exciting electronic & different styles of contemporary music coming out of India & the subcontinent. They focus on mood enhancing sound design and poetic lyrics. They play the guitar, piano and violin and incorporate these organic sounds with electronic components. They are inspired by Radiohead, Billie Eilish and The Marias etc.
