Hidden gem of the week
Flavours of the east w/ alboe & merak

Flavours of the East is a radio show on Worldwide FM presented by Krunk Kulture, a record label based outta India. The show aims to showcase the most exciting electronic & different styles of contemporary music coming out of India & the subcontinent. Krunk Kulture is part of Krunk (www.krunklive.com), one of India's oldest running events & artist management agencies pushing electronic and live music in India since 2009. Krunk Kulture is operated by DJ / Producer / & Label head, Sohail Arora.

This episode is hosted by two Krunk Kulture label artists, Alboe & Merak. First up: We start with Merak are a brother - sister duo based out of Goa, India. Merak is an electronic music duo of siblings Brandon and Belinda Mendes. They focus on mood enhancing sound design and poetic lyrics. They play the guitar, piano and violin and incorporate these organic sounds with electronic components. They are inspired by Radiohead, Billie Eilish and The Marias etc.

Second Hour is presented by Alboe. A one hour set playing Alboe’s latest album as well as other recent tunes emerging from New Delhi. The session is a mix of the experimental sounds as well pop & melodic electronic music. Artists include, Alboe, Kenzani and Tech Panda, Dhruv Visvanath and Synths Back.

Vedant Chandra or Alboe (A little Bit of Everything) is a New Delhi based singer-songwriter, music producer and DJ. Alboe, who has been a regular on the Delhi electronic dance music circuit has been developing his ""live"" alter ego for the last couple of years with numerous independently released tracks and performances. Formerly known as Iam ALBOE, he releases this incredible 10 track debut album, ‘The Painter', under his new moniker, Alboe on 23rd July 2021.
